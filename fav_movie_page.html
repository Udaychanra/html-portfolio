1.Khaleja

Khaleja is a 2010 Indian Telugu-language fantasy action comedy film starring Mahesh Babu and Anushka Shetty in the lead roles and directed by Trivikram Srinivas. It was produced by Singanamala Ramesh and C Kalyan, with Mahesh Babu's sister Manjula Ghattamaneni as the co-producer. The film features music composed by Mani Sharma with cinematography by Yash Bhardwaj and editing by A Sreekar Prasad. The film was released on 7 October 2010 to positive reviews from critics and became a commercial success.

2.KGF

K.G.F: Chapter 1 is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film written and directed by Prashanth Neel, and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under the banner Hombale films. It is the first installment in the two-part series, followed by K.G.F: Chapter 2. The film stars Yash in the lead role as Rocky, while Ramachandra Raju features as Garuda. Anant Nag narrates the film while also playing a supporting role alongside Srinidhi Shetty, Vasishta N. Simha, Ayyappa P. Sharma and Harish Rai. The narrative follows the rise of a gangster named Rocky, who aims to control the gold mines of Kolar, and his relationship with a reporter who intends to expose his true self.
