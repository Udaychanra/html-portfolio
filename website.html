India take on Australia in the final of the World Cup 2023. Both teams are on a tremendous winning streak, going into the final of the tournament, set to be played on 19 November, Sunday in Ahmedabad's Narendra Modi Stadium. While India have won 10 matches in a row and are the only unbeaten team in this tournament, Australia have won their last 8, after starting with two defeats. World Cup-winner Yuvraj Singh has backed the Indian team to win the tournament. Yuvraj believes that the Indian cricket team has never seen a more balanced side. The former cricketer, while speaking on Sports Tak said that the team has 8-10 match winners, which was only seen in the Australian cricket team when they were dominating cricket between 1999-2007. / MUST READ Video: Biden 'wanders off' at G7 Summit, pulled back by Italian PM Meloni TRENDING TOPICS: J&K Attacks NEET Row T20 World Cup Modi Government 3.0 "I have never seen a better combination in the Indian team. You have 5 batters, 8-10 match winners in a single team. Australian team used to have this kind of prowess when they were dominating world cricket in 2003-2007," Yuvraj Singh said on Sports Tak. Asked about senior batter Virat Kohli, who is having a stunning tournament, Yuvraj said that he backs Kohli to break Sachin Tendulkar's record of 100 centuries. The former India batter said that at the pace that Virat Kohli is going, anything could be possible. "At the pace that Virat Kohli is going, he should break Sachin Tendulkar's record of 100 centuries. In ODI cricket especially, I think he can score more hundreds. He has a tremendous conversion rate. 71 fifties and 50 hundreds is not a joke," Yuvraj Singh said about Virat Kohli. Playing against New Zealand at the Wankhede Stadium, Virat Kohli surpassed Sachin Tendulkar's of 49 ODI hundreds. The batter has 80 hundreds in international cricket right now, 20 short of 'The Master'. Kohli is currently the highest run-getter in the World Cup 2023 with 711 runs to his name.
